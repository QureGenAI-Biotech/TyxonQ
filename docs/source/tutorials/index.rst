Overview
========

Our tutorials are organized into three progressive levels:

🌱 **Beginner**: Start here if you're new to quantum computing  
🌿 **Intermediate**: Ready for variational algorithms and chemistry  
🌲 **Advanced**: Cutting-edge techniques and research applications  

Learning Path
=============

**Recommended progression**:

1. **Start with Beginner** → Learn quantum basics, gates, and circuits
2. **Move to Intermediate** → Master VQE, optimization, and quantum chemistry  
3. **Advance to Expert** → Explore hybrid algorithms and quantum ML

**Time Investment**:
- Beginner: 2-4 hours
- Intermediate: 4-6 hours  
- Advanced: 6-8 hours

What You'll Learn
=================

Beginner Tutorial
-----------------

✅ **Installation & Setup**: Get TyxonQ running  
✅ **First Quantum Circuit**: Bell states and entanglement  
✅ **Basic Gates**: X, H, CNOT operations  
✅ **Multi-Qubit Systems**: GHZ states and scaling  
✅ **Simulators**: Choosing the right backend  
✅ **Practical Exercises**: Hands-on practice  

**Perfect for**: Complete beginners, students, educators

Intermediate Tutorial
---------------------

✅ **Variational Algorithms**: VQE from scratch  
✅ **Quantum Chemistry**: H₂ and molecular calculations  
✅ **Optimization Techniques**: Gradients and methods  
✅ **Algorithm Comparison**: UCCSD vs HEA vs PUCCD  
✅ **Custom Implementation**: Build your own VQE  
✅ **Real Projects**: Practical applications  

**Perfect for**: Those with basic quantum knowledge, chemistry researchers

Advanced Tutorial
-----------------

✅ **Hybrid Quantum-Classical**: Neural networks with quantum layers  
✅ **Quantum Machine Learning**: Kernels, SVM, generative models  
✅ **Advanced VQE**: Adaptive ansatz construction  
✅ **Error Correction**: Basic quantum error correction codes  
✅ **Research Applications**: Cutting-edge techniques  
✅ **Performance Optimization**: Efficient implementations  

**Perfect for**: Researchers, advanced practitioners, algorithm developers
